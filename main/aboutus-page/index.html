Our Story

Choco Hoho was founded in 2025 out of our passion for crafting delightful chocolates and sweets to share with everyone. From the very beginning, we have been dedicated to using high-quality ingredients and hand-crafted methods to bring out the true flavor of every piece. At Choco Hoho, we believe that each product carries a story of love and joy, made to be shared with family, friends, and all cake lovers. Visit us, and you'll discover that every bite is a sweet moment worth remembering.

What makes Choco HoHo truly special is the story behind it. The shop was created by three close friends who met during their university years—three boys who discovered they shared the same dream: to build a bakery that could spread happiness through simple, homemade bread. Between long lectures, late-night study sessions, and countless conversations, they realized their shared passion for baking and decided to turn it into something meaningful. What began as small experiments in a tiny dorm kitchen slowly grew into a vision of a warm, joyful bakery where people could enjoy fresh bread every day.

Together, we combined our strengths, creativity, and love for crafting delicious treats to bring Choco Hoho to life. Every loaf we bake carries the spirit of friendship, dedication, and youthful enthusiasm that started it all. And just like our founders, we hope our bread brings people together—creating moments to laugh, share, and enjoy life's sweetness.
